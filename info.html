abun.ch

Shorten multiple links at once.

This is a tool to compile a list of multiple links, called a bunch, under a short URL with features like an optional description, password, link titles, automatic expiry and more.

It is very much a work in progress and the creation of new bunches is currently limited to owners of an account.

You can check out a demo of a bunch here and create new bunches at https://abun.ch/new.
